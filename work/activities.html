   Erasmus+ IT CAREER START-UP Erasmus+ Project, 2017-1-RO01-KA102-036700, Beneficiary “Gheorghe Vranceanu” National College, Bacau, Romania was funded by the European Commission, facilitating inter-institutional cooperation between partner institutions, 4 schools and 16 companies from Cyprus, Spain and Portugal, in order to support students’ career orientation towards the IT sector. As such, Universal Mobility S.L, Valencia, intermediary organization for Spain, enabled a 3-week practical training (09.07.2018-27.07.2018) within 8 IT companies for 16 Romanian students.

   MBB Electronics S.L., Droid House S.L., Asociacion Cultural TotArt S.L, Eventos Samsara SL, Dynos Informática S.L., Inforlandia S.L., Mundotec SL, Idea y Diseña S.L provided student placements aimed at acquiring professional skills for creating web pages using Adobe Dreamweaver. The recognition of the acquired competences was done through the Europass Mobility document.

   The project had a positive impact on the participants, who had a first European mobility experience and acquired professional, personal, civic, intercultural and linguistic skills by working in a multicultural team and context. In the long run, they will be more competitive on the labour market, being more prepared and motivated to choose an IT career in Romania or abroad.
