  Totart Scenic Arts Academy, Wellness School, Multimedia and AudioVisual, is an independent art center in Valencia, whose purpose is to stimulate the cultural activity of the city of Valencia and offer the citizens the posibility to benefit from a multicultural tutoring space.

  Specific activities: graphic design and animation services for different events, using new technologies, web design, theater for children, preparation for the E.S.A.D. test, aerial fabrics, intensive workshops with actors, actresses and directors or film directors, summer cinema school.


-Club TotArt-

  Do you want to be part of our club? Do you want to have a space to go to write, to exchange opinions, share projects and meet people with whom to develop them? That's Club Totart . A place where you will also receive advice on your script, generate work circles, get discounts on courses at the school, rent rooms to carry out your tests and rent materials for your projects. With a monthly cineforum and weekly meetings in which we will expose the projects and create work teams to develop them, and a job board. And all this for a monthly fee of 30 euros .
